Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Introduction

DeepBreath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

  • What: Your device's GPS location
  • Why: To show nearby air quality data and verify merchant locations
  • When: Only while using the app
  • Storage: Not stored on our servers for regular users

2. Camera Data

  • What: Photos taken within the app
  • Why: Merchants upload photos of air quality monitors as verification
  • Storage: Photos are stored on Firebase servers and automatically deleted after 3 days

3. Air Quality Data (Merchants Only)

  • What: Business name, address, contact info, PM2.5/PM10 readings
  • Why: To display air quality information to users
  • Storage: Stored on Firebase servers, automatically deleted after 3 days

How We Use Your Information

  • Display nearby indoor air quality information
  • Verify merchant submissions are from actual business locations
  • Moderate uploaded images for inappropriate content
  • Improve our services

Data Security

  • All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption
  • Images are automatically scanned for inappropriate content
  • Data is automatically deleted after 3 days
  • We use Google Firebase for secure data storage
